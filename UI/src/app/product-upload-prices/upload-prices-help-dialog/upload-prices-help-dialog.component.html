dashboard Page Layout

The Product Promotions page has three sections side by side:

filter_list Search & Filter

Find existing promotions in the data grid.

Choose a Search By option (ID, Customer, Item ID, Promo Start/End Date, or Upload Date), enter your value, and click Search.

cloud_upload Upload

Upload new promotional prices:

1. Select ERP Customer ID

2. Choose your CSV file

3. Click Upload

eSyncMate validates each row and applies promotional prices based on start/end dates.

build Actions

Has its own independent ERP Customer ID selector.

Sample — Download a CSV template with correct column headers.

Discrepancies — Download a report showing price differences between your uploads and the marketplace.

lightbulb The Upload and Actions sections have independent Customer ID selections. Selecting a customer in Upload does not affect the Actions section. Only CSV files are accepted for upload.

touch_app What You Can Do

These actions are available on the Product Promotions page:

cloud_upload
Upload Promotional Prices

In the Upload section, select a customer, choose your CSV file, and click Upload. eSyncMate validates each row and schedules promotional prices based on the start and end dates you provided.

file_download
Download Sample Template

In the Actions section, click Sample to download a CSV template with the correct column headers: Item ID, Promo List Price, Offer Price, MAP Price, Start Date, and End Date.

download
Download Discrepancies Report

In the Actions section, select a customer and click Discrepancies. eSyncMate generates a report showing differences between your uploaded prices and the prices currently on the marketplace.

format_list_numbered Step-by-Step Upload Process

Follow these steps to upload promotional prices:

1
Select Customer In the Upload section, choose the ERP Customer ID for the marketplace partner
2
Download Template In the Actions section, click Sample to get a CSV file with the correct column headers
3
Fill In Your Data Open the template in Excel and enter Item ID, promo prices, start date, and end date for each product
4
Upload File In the Upload section, choose your completed CSV file and click Upload
5
Verify Results Use Search to find your customer's promotions in the grid. Check that prices and dates are correct
lightbulb After uploading, use the Discrepancies report in the Actions section to verify that marketplace prices match your uploads. If there are differences, re-upload the corrected file.

view_list Grid Columns

The data grid shows these columns for each promotional price record:

badge
Customer ID / Item ID The marketplace partner and product item number
attach_money
Promo Prices List Price, Offer Price, and MAP Price for the promotion period
date_range
Promo Start / End Date Date and time when the promotion begins and ends (MM/DD/YYYY hh:mm AM/PM)
history
Original Prices / Upload Date The original prices before the promotion was applied, and when it was uploaded
