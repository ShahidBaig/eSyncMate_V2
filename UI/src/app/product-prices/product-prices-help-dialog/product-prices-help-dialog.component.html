dashboard Page Layout

The Product Prices page has three sections side by side:

filter_list Search & Filter

Find existing price records in the data grid.

Choose a Search By option from the dropdown, enter your value, and click Search.

cloud_upload Upload

Upload new pricing data:

1. Select ERP Customer ID

2. Choose your CSV file

3. Click Upload

eSyncMate validates and imports the prices.

Only visible if you have Add permission.

build Actions

Has its own independent Customer ID.

Sample — Download CSV template.

Process Prices — Send prices to marketplace.

lightbulb Upload and Actions have independent Customer ID selections. Selecting a customer in Upload does not affect Actions, and vice versa. Only CSV files are accepted for upload.

touch_app What You Can Do

These actions are available on the Product Prices page:

cloud_upload
Upload Prices

In the Upload section, select a customer, choose your CSV file, and click Upload. eSyncMate validates each row and imports the pricing data. Only visible if you have Add permission.

file_download
Download Sample Template

In the Actions section, select a customer and click Sample to download a CSV template with the correct column headers for pricing data.

sync
Process Prices

In the Actions section, select a customer and click Process Prices. eSyncMate triggers the price sync to send the pricing data to the marketplace partner.

table_chart
Export Excel

Click the Export Excel button above the data grid to download the current grid data as an Excel file for offline review or reporting.

format_list_numbered Step-by-Step Upload Process

Follow these steps to upload pricing data:

1
Download Template In the Actions section, select the customer and click Sample to download the CSV template with the correct column headers
2
Fill In Your Prices Open the downloaded template in Excel, fill in the pricing data for your items, and save as CSV
3
Upload File In the Upload section, select the same customer, choose your completed CSV file, and click Upload
4
Verify Upload After a successful upload, the grid refreshes automatically. Your uploaded prices will appear with status NEW
5
Process & Sync In the Actions section, select the customer and click Process Prices to send pricing to the marketplace
lightbulb Always download a fresh template before uploading to ensure you have the latest column format. Make sure Item IDs exist in your product catalog before uploading prices.

label Pricing Statuses

Each pricing record has a color-coded status that tracks its progress through the pricing lifecycle. Hover over any status in the grid to see a tooltip with more details.

fiber_newNEW

When set: Immediately after a price is uploaded via CSV file.

What it means: The product price has been uploaded and saved in eSyncMate, but has not yet been published or sent to the marketplace.

check_circleAPPROVED

When set: After the product data has been validated and published.

What it means: The product data is published and the pricing is ready to be processed and sent to the marketplace.

price_checkAPPROVED_PR

When set: After the pricing information has been specifically processed and synced.

What it means: The product pricing information has been synced. This is an intermediate step between APPROVED and SYNCED, confirming that pricing data was successfully transmitted.

cloud_doneSYNCED

When set: After the complete product and pricing data has been successfully sent to the marketplace partner.

What it means: The product is fully synced with the marketplace. The retailer now has the latest pricing. Hover the status to see which marketplace partner it was synced with.

updateUPDATED

When set: When an existing price record is modified with new pricing values.

What it means: The pricing has been changed since the last sync. The updated price will need to be processed again to push the changes to the marketplace.

delete_outlineDELETED

When set: When a pricing record has been marked for removal.

What it means: The pricing record is no longer active. It has been removed from the sync process and will not be sent to the marketplace.
