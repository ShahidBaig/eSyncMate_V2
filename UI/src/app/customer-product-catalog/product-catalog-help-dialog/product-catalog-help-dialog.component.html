cloud_upload Upload Mode (Default)

This is the default view. Use the Upload pill button on the right side of the page title to switch to this mode. It has three sections side by side:

filter_list Search & Filter

Find existing products in the data grid below.

Choose a Search By option (Product ID, Customer ID, Item ID, Status, Item Type, or Date), enter your value, and click Search.

The results appear in the product grid with all columns — Customer ID, Item ID, UPC, Status, Prices, and Upload Date.

cloud_upload Upload

Upload new product data to eSyncMate:

1. Select ERP Customer ID

2. Select Item Type

3. Choose your CSV file

4. Click Upload

eSyncMate validates each row and adds valid products to the catalog. Invalid rows are flagged as rejected.

build Actions

Has its own independent Customer ID and Item Type selectors.

Sample — Download a CSV template with correct column headers for the selected item type.

Rejected — Download products that failed validation so you can fix and re-upload them.

Clear Errors — Reset products stuck in Error status so eSyncMate can retry processing them.

lightbulb Each section (Search, Upload, Actions) has its own independent Customer ID and Item Type selection. Changing the customer in Upload does not affect Actions or Download.

download Download Mode

Click the Download pill button on the right side of the page title to switch to this mode:

download Download Product Data

Export your existing product data as a CSV file to your computer.

1. Select ERP Customer ID — choose which marketplace partner's products to download.

2. Select Item Type — choose the product category.

3. Click Download CSV — eSyncMate exports all matching products as a CSV file.

lightbulb The product grid and search controls are hidden in Download mode. The Download section uses its own Customer ID and Item Type — independent from Upload and Actions. Switch back to Upload to view, search, and manage products.

label Understanding Product Statuses

Each product has a color-coded status so you can track its progress at a glance:

fiber_newNew

Product has been uploaded to eSyncMate and is waiting to be sent to the marketplace for approval.

check_circleApproved

The marketplace has approved the product. It is now ready for pricing and inventory sync.

publishPublished

Product is live on the marketplace. Customers can now see and purchase this product.

updateUpdated

Product details have been modified since the last publish. eSyncMate will send the changes on the next sync.

hourglass_emptyPending

Product is waiting for marketplace approval. This usually happens after initial upload or after updates.

cancelRejected

The marketplace rejected this product. Download the rejected items CSV to see the reason, fix the data, and re-upload.

errorError

Something went wrong during processing. Use Clear Errors in the Actions section to reset and retry.

visibility_offUnlisted

Product has been removed from the marketplace listing. It is no longer visible to customers but still exists in eSyncMate.

cloud_upload Upload Section

In Upload mode, select your customer and item type, then upload your product CSV file:

cloud_upload
Upload Products

Select an ERP Customer ID and Item Type, choose your CSV file, and click Upload. eSyncMate validates each row and adds valid products to the catalog.

lightbulb Only CSV files are accepted for upload. Make sure your column headers match the sample template exactly.

build Actions Section

The Actions panel shows the currently selected customer and item type, with these buttons:

file_download
Download Sample Template

Download a pre-formatted CSV template with the correct column headers for the selected customer and item type. Use this as your starting point for data entry.

error_outline
Download Rejected Products

Download a CSV of products that failed validation (missing fields, invalid UPC, duplicate entries, etc.). Fix the issues and re-upload.

auto_fix_high
Clear Errors

Reset all products stuck in Error status so eSyncMate can reprocess them. This clears the error flag and allows the system to retry.

download Download Mode

Switch to Download mode using the toggle at the top to export your product data:

download
Download CSV

Select the ERP Customer ID and Item Type, then click Download CSV. eSyncMate will export all matching product data as a CSV file to your computer.

format_list_numbered Step-by-Step Upload Process

Follow these steps to upload products successfully:

1
Select Customer Choose the ERP Customer ID for the marketplace partner you are uploading products for
2
Choose Item Type Select the product category. This determines which columns appear in the template
3
Download Template Click the Sample button in the Actions section to get a CSV with the correct column headers
4
Fill in Your Data Open the template in Excel and fill in your product information (item IDs, pricing, UPC, etc.)
5
Upload File In the Upload section, choose your completed CSV file and click Upload
6
Review Results Valid products appear in the grid. If any rows failed, click Rejected in Actions to download and review errors
lightbulb Always download the latest template before uploading. Column names must match exactly or the upload will fail.
